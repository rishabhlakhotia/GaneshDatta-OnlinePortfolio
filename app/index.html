Hello

I'm Ganesh Datta

Graduate in Bachelors of Commerce from Jain University, in 2014. Got into the field of training and education and started a firm called “THE PROJECT-7 TEAM” which is into life skills, motivation and self-development. Helping students to overcome fear and build their inter-personal skills. Making them realize how important it is to think out of the box, be creative and understand themselves better.


Believing in revolutionizing our education system, GD started by organising workshops on personality development, team work and leadership to students aged between 14-22 years. Since 19th January 2013 GD has completed workshops on leadership, personality development and motivation to over 4000+ students across various schools and colleges, some of them being at Jain College Jayanagar(PU+UG+PG), Jain college V.V.Puram(PU+UG), Jain College J.C.Road(PU+UG), Jain University, V.E.T. School, St. Marks School, Christ Junior College, Christ University, Maharaja Sayyajirao University – Baroda, Ahmedabad University, Kammavari Sangha Institute of Technology (KSIT), Sai Ram College of Engineering, SRCE, Doon International School (Dehradun), National Institute of Engineering (NIE Mysuru) and many more.
